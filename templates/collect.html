Collect Images

There are two methods for collecting images:
  • Still Shots: In this method, you will treat the video camera like a regular camera and take still shots manually. Just press "Take Photo" to take a picture.
  • Motion Capture: This method will activate the video camera and monitor for motion. When motion is detected, it will automatically take a picture. It will also save the coordinates of the detected object which can be used during annotation.
